# The Admin Panel

Even a minimal CMS needs some kind of admin interface. Here's what the TeenyTinyCMS admin does.

## What It Handles

- Login / logout with session-based auth
- Triggering a full rebuild
- Viewing build output and stats

## What It Doesn't Do

- Edit content — that's your text editor's job
- Manage users — admin credentials live in config

Keeping the admin tiny means there's less surface area to secure.
