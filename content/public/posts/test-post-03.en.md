# Why File-Based CMS?

Traditional database-driven CMS systems are powerful but complex. A file-based approach keeps things simple.

## Benefits

1. No database setup required
2. Content is version-controllable via Git
3. Easy to back up — just copy the folder

When your site is small, simplicity wins.
