# Multilingual Sites Made Simple

Supporting multiple languages doesn't have to be a nightmare.

## The Approach

Each piece of content exists as a separate file per language:

- `about.en.md`
- `about.de.md`
- `about.fr.md`

The language switcher links them together via the `slug` field in the front matter.

## No Magic Required

No plugin, no complicated routing. Just consistent naming and a bit of convention.
