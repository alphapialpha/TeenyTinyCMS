# The Case for SQLite

SQLite gets a bad reputation for "serious" applications. That reputation is mostly undeserved.

## What SQLite Is Great For

- Single-user applications
- Small to medium websites
- Development environments
- Embedded systems

## A Real Statistic

SQLite is likely the most widely deployed database engine in the world — it's in your phone, your browser, and countless apps.

For a personal blog or small CMS, SQLite is more than enough.
