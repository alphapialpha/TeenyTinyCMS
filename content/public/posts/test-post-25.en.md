# When Teeny Is Enough

Not every project needs a full content platform. Sometimes small is exactly right.

## Signs You Should Stay Teeny

- Fewer than 10 editors
- No real-time content requirements
- You prefer editing in your text editor
- You value Git-controlled content
- You don't want to maintain a bloated plugin ecosystem

## Signs You Should Move On

- You need a visual drag-and-drop editor
- Non-technical editors will be creating content daily
- You need live user-generated content

Know your requirements. Teeny is enough more often than you think.
